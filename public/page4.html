Difference in Care

Sree Datta Hospitals was established in 2011 by Dr. Rajesh Kappala, renowned Diabetist in Nizamabad.
Constructed in the 2011, the Center is equipped with the most-up-to-date seismic-safety features and high-tech medical apparatus as well as a sophisticated and energy-efficient climate control system designed to maximize the comfort of doctors, patients and staff.

Dedicated to offering health care services that are welcoming, accessible, safe and respectful for everyone, Sree Datta Hospitals has engaged patients, families and community representatives and leaders as active partners to ensure that their voices are heard and included in every area of its operations.

The Value that drive us
