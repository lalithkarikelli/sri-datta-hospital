Difference in Care

Sri Datta Multi Speciality Hospital was established in 2008 by Dr. Rajesh Kappala, renowned Diabetologist in Nizamabad.
The Center is equipped with the most-up-to-date seismic-safety features and high-tech medical apparatus as well as a sophisticated and energy-efficient climate control system designed to maximize the comfort of doctors, patients and staff.

Dedicated to offering health care services that are welcoming, accessible, safe and respectful for everyone, Sri Datta Multi Speciality Hospital has engaged patients, families and community representatives and leaders as active partners to ensure that their voices are heard and included in every area of its operations.

The Value that drive us

Sri Datta Multi Speciality Hospital was among the first few hospitals in the city to leverage technology to facilitate seamless healthcare delivery.

Our Vision

Strive to be the community's preferred healthcare partner. Also, to be recognized as the greatest health-care service provider with high-quality standards.

Our Mission

To provide high quality, affordable health care services through integrated clinical practice and high end technology.

Team Work

Teamwork begins by building trust. And the only way to do that is to overcome our need for invulnerability.
